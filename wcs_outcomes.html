How outcomes for body camera charges have changed over time
Body camera charges peaked in 2021 with suspensions making up a majority of outcomes. Since 2022, non-disciplinary reinstructions have surged — accounting for nearly half of all outcomes in 2023.
Source: Cleveland Division of Police disciplinary records, 2017-2025. Each case represents one officer charged with a Wearable Camera System (WCS) violation. Cases with multiple charges are counted once under the most severe outcome.
